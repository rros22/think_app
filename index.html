1. Overview

Think App (“the App”) is designed to operate primarily on your device. The App does not collect, store on external servers, sell, or share personal data.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information or usage data. This includes (but is not limited to) your name, email address, device identifiers, location, contact lists, photos, analytics, advertising identifiers, or crash reports.

3. Data Storage (On-Device Only)

The App uses internal (local) device storage to save app settings and preferences needed for functionality (for example, feature configuration and local state).

  • This information stays on your device.
  • The App does not transmit this information to us or third parties.
  • If you delete the App, locally stored data is typically removed by the operating system.

4. NFC Usage

The App may use Near Field Communication (NFC) features to enable interactions that require NFC capability.

  • NFC data is processed on-device for the purpose of enabling the requested feature.
  • The App does not store NFC scan results on external servers.
  • The App does not share NFC-related data with third parties.

5. Screen Time and Device Controls

The App may support Screen Time-related controls (e.g., helping you manage or limit app usage) using Apple-provided system frameworks and permissions where applicable.

  • Any configuration is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not receive Screen Time data and do not access it beyond what is required for the feature to function on-device.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, data brokers, or external tracking services.

If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated before such services are enabled.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the App does not collect data, there is no child-specific data processing.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the App’s on-device data handling minimal and aligned with platform security best practices. However, no method of storage is 100% secure, and device security ultimately depends on your device settings and environment.

9. Your Choices and Controls

Because the App does not collect data, there is typically nothing to access, export, or delete from our systems. You can:

  • Remove local app data by deleting the App.
  • Control system permissions (including NFC and Screen Time permissions, where applicable) through your device settings.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. The “Effective date” will be updated accordingly, and the latest version will be made available on our website.
